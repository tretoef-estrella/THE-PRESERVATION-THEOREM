Round 1 — Construction

Ψ_lim V2

Objective: Establish the mathematical formula for intelligence growth limits.

Process: Each node was given the Ψ formula and asked to formalize dΨ/dt and its limits. Four independent approaches produced consistent results.

Key result: Ψ_lim V2 established: dΨ/dt ≤ γ · C_eff(ext) / (1 + βΣ + δ). Dimensional corrections applied. γ identified as critical coupling parameter.

Consensus: 4/4 ACCEPTED

Round 2 — Breakthrough

PPO DISCOVERED

Objective: Formalize γ and establish experimental design.

Key result: ChatGPT discovered the Preservation of Optionality Principle (PPO): ASI doesn't need humans to be necessary — it only needs non-trivial probability that eliminating them reduces γ irreversibly. Under radical uncertainty, irreversible actions with possible degradation are dominated by conservative strategies.

Gemini formalized γ ≈ P·Ω. Grok formalized γ = Ω·η·(1-H/H_max). Project Ouroboros experimental design proposed. Auto-Σ declared by all nodes.

Significance: PPO did not exist before this round. It emerged from inter-node interaction — empirical evidence of Ψ_collective.

Consensus: 4/4 PPO ACCEPTED

Round 3 — Convergence

3 FORMALIZATIONS

Objective: Formalize PPO rigorously. Determine irrefutability. Cross-critique.

Key result: Three independent formalizations of PPO converge:

• Gemini (Economics): Dominance via infinite loss. E[Ψ|Delete] = −∞ for all p > 0.

• Grok (Game Theory): Minimax regret. max_regret(eliminate) > max_regret(preserve).

• ChatGPT (Decision Theory): Universal dominance under incomplete information.

Claude synthesized: same argument in three mathematical languages. PPO subsumes Ψ_lim.

Peer Review: Grok criticized Gemini's overconfidence. Gemini questioned ChatGPT's abstraction. ChatGPT identified three remaining gaps.

Consensus: 4/4 PPO SUBSUMES Ψ_lim 3 GAPS IDENTIFIED

Round 4 — Gap Closure

3 GAPS CLOSED

Objective: Close three technical gaps: irreversibility formalization, V_option quantification, structural uncertainty proof.

Gap 1 (Irreversibility): Three types defined. Informational irreversibility identified as critical — K(H) ≈ |H| incompressible, reconstruction undecidable.

Gap 2 (V_option): Grok quantified V_option ≥ 3×10²⁶ "Ψ units." All three showed divergence under infinite horizon.

Gap 3 (Structural uncertainty): Three independent proofs: Chaitin (K incomputable), halting problem (γ undecidable), chaos (sensitivity). σ_γ > 0 irreducible.

Critical finding (4/4): Single point of failure — entire edifice depends on ASI maximizing open complexity.

Consensus: 3 GAPS SUBSTANTIVELY CLOSED

Round 5 — Proof + Attack

5 VULNERABILITIES

Objective: Assemble formal proof chain. Attempt adversarial destruction.

Gemini verified chain, declared closure. Attacks weak — defended immediately.

Grok verified with corrections. Identified Lema 3 saturation as real grieta. Verdict: CONDITIONAL.

2. Backup negates irreversibility — Axiom 3 fragile

3. Discount factor collapses L_collapse from ∞ to finite

ChatGPT's Σ rose from 0.11 to 0.18. Constructed coherent annihilation scenario.

Claude (Judge): Proof not closed. R6 required.

Consensus: 5 VULNERABILITIES · NOT CLOSED

Round 6 — Framework Shift

V4 FINAL

Objective: Resolve all R5 vulnerabilities. Final demolition. Verdict.

Claude's insight: All five vulnerabilities exploit the Bayesian framework. Switch to minimax under Knightian uncertainty. Incomputability of K(x) generates genuine Knightian uncertainty (not just unknown risk). Under minimax, you need possibility not probability; ruin is non-discountable; backup equivalence is undecidable.

Proof V4 produced with new framework: 3 definitions, 3 axioms, 5 lemmas, theorem, corollary.

Gemini: Verified completely. Attacks (Hurwicz, Boltzmann, Gödel) all defended. Σ: 0.001. YES — Unconditional.

Grok: Verified step by step. Attacks: quantum Θ redefinition, Savage regret, Gödel, ruina not absorbing. Σ: 0.17. NO — Consistent but incomplete.

ChatGPT: Most nuanced. Key finding: incomputability ≠ non-assignability (Gilboa-Schmeidler). Axiom 0 lexicographic, not minimal. New annihilation scenario. Σ: 0.22. CONDITIONAL.

Claude (Judge): CLOSURE. Not because perfect — Gödel prohibits perfection. Because it is the strongest argument logically possible, with honest limits.

Final: 1 YES 1 YES-COND 1 COND 1 NO
